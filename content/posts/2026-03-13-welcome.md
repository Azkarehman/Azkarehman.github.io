## What is this?

This blog automatically curates and highlights the latest research papers in **medical imaging** and **AI** — covering topics like segmentation, classification, regression, and new developments in Vision-Language Models (VLMs), CNNs, and Transformers.

## What to expect

Every **Monday and Thursday**, a new digest will be posted here featuring:

- **Recent papers** from PubMed and arXiv matching key topics in medical AI
- **Brief summaries** of what each paper contributes
- **Why it matters** for clinical applications and research

## Topics covered

- Medical image segmentation, classification, and detection
- Foundation models and VLMs for healthcare
- CNN and Transformer architecture innovations
- Survival analysis and risk prediction
- Explainability (Grad-CAM, attention maps) in clinical AI

Stay tuned for the first research digest!
